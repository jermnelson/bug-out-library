Suitcase Library

What happens if we have to recreate our culture and civilization from a portable library? What if we were all refugees from some calamity and all we had to bootstrap ourselves in a constrained, transportable physical object? What should be in that?

An ongoing art project in creating an "end-of-world" or a "bug out bag" library of conventional and electronic resources, all constrained by the size of medium roller suitcase.
